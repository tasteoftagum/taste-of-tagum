Must Try

Kwek-Kwek

A Tagum staple. Orange-battered quail eggs, deep fried to a perfect crisp and served with spiced vinegar. A ritual, not just a snack.

₱20-25 per piece
Local Favorite

Proben

Chicken proventriculus on a stick, deep fried and dipped in suka tuba with garlic and chili. Five pesos. Unforgettable. The soul of Tagum street food.

₱5 per stick
fishball photo
Classic

Fishball

Five pesos, a stick, and the best sweet-spicy sauce you'll find anywhere. Simple, honest, and always exactly what you need at 5pm.

₱1 per stick
